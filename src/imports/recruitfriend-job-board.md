Design a comprehensive, modern recruitment job board platform called 
"RecruitFriend" for the South African market. The brand concept is 
"your friendly guide to finding work" — approachable, warm, and 
professional. Like a trusted friend who happens to know every job 
in South Africa. Three user types: Job Seekers, Employers, and Admins.

---

BRAND IDENTITY

Name: RecruitFriend
Tagline: "Your Career. Our Mission."
Brand Personality: Friendly, trustworthy, energetic, professional — 
  like a helpful career coach in your pocket.
Logo concept: Wordmark "RecruitFriend" where the word "Friend" is 
  in the accent green, with a small handshake or people-connecting 
  icon to the left.

Colour Palette:
- Primary: #0A2540 (deep navy — trust, professionalism)
- Accent: #00C853 (vibrant green — growth, opportunity, friendliness)
- Warm highlight: #FF6B35 (orange — energy, warmth, CTAs for seekers)
- Background: #F5F7FA (light grey)
- Card: #FFFFFF
- Border: #E5E7EB
- Text primary: #111827
- Text secondary: #6B7280
- Success: #10B981 | Warning: #F59E0B | Error: #EF4444

Typography:
- Headings: "Sora" or "DM Sans" — rounded, modern, friendly
- Body: "Inter" or "Nunito" — clean and highly readable
- Accent labels/badges: Semi-bold, uppercase, letter-spaced

Design Language:
- Rounded corners everywhere (12px cards, 16px large panels, 
  999px pills/badges)
- Soft shadows (not harsh) — 0 2px 8px rgba(0,0,0,0.08)
- Friendly micro-illustrations and empty state art
- Warm gradients: navy-to-green for hero sections
- Green underline accent on key headings
- "RF" monogram badge for avatar placeholders
- Sticky top navigation on all screens
